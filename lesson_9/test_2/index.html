Roy Batty, 2019

Like Tears in Rain

Where am I going? How long have I got?

've seen things you people wouldn't believe. Attack ships on fire off
the shoulder of Orion. I watched C-beams glitter in the
dark near the Tannhauser gate. All those moments will be lost in time... like tears
in rain... Time to die.

I don't know why he saved my life. Maybe in those last moments he
loved life more than he ever had before. Not just his life - anybody's
life; my life. All he'd wanted were the same answers...
